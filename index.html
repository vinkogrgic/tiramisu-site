The hard truth

Most founders don't fail to raise because the business is bad.

Why not just use a tool

You'll soon have no shortage of cheaper options: auto raise-readiness apps, AI agents that grade your deck in seconds, junior operators and investors running the same playbook as free lead-gen. Most do the same thing: feed your raise into a generator and forward you the output.

That's the mistake. A raise lives or dies on whether the numbers hold up under an investor's scrutiny, and a model that looks right but is quietly wrong is worse than none at all.

I use the best AI tools to work faster and dig deeper for you. But judgment is built over years inside the room and hundreds of deals of pattern recognition, and that can't be generated. I check every sheet and every document with my own eyes, especially the financial model and unit economics.
